# General
Frequently Asked Questions

## Why isn't Tsundoku on the Google Play Store?
**Tsundoku** won't be on the **Google Play Store**.

APK-based extensions conflict with [Google Play's content policy](https://play.google.com/about/developer-content-policy/).
**Google** might take down the app due to certain content, which the developers wishes to avoid.

## Is Tsundoku available for iOS/iPadOS?
There is no iOS or iPadOS version and neither are there plans for one.
Porting is difficult due to the separate codebases of iOS and Android apps.

Any app proclaiming to be "**Tsundoku for iOS**" is not by us and should be treated as a scam.

## What is Tsundoku Beta?
**Tsundoku Beta** (**囤β**) is the latest preview version of the app.
It showcases potential upcoming features, but it's more prone to bugs and crashes.

::: warning CAUTION
While it's ideal for users seeking the latest **Tsundoku** experience, it's essential to keep [auto-backup](/docs/guides/backups#enabling-automatic-backups) enabled to prevent library loss due to issues.
:::

## Can I read light novels?
**Tsundoku** can read light novels; it's a text parser.

## Can I read manga?
**Tsundoku** can read manga; it inherits most of the features of Mihon..

## Can I stream anime?
**Tsundoku** isn't designed for anime streaming.

Projects using the **Tsundoku** name for anime streaming aren't affiliated with the main project.

## What's a fork?
Forks are alternate **Tsundoku** versions with distinct features.
Get more details [here on forks](/forks/).
